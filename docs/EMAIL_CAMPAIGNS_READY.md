## ðŸ’¡ **PRO TIPS**

### **1. Start Small**
- Test with 5-10 leads first
- Monitor results
- Adjust templates
- Scale up gradually

### **2. Personalize Templates**
- Replace `[YOUR VALUE PROPOSITION]` with your actual value
- Add real examples and case studies
- Make it specific to your business

### **3. Best Send Times**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 10 AM - 11 AM
- Tuesday-Thursday: 2 PM - 3 PM
- Avoid Mondays, Fridays, weekends

- **Good Open Rate:** >20%
- **Good Reply Rate:** >5%
- **Good Bounce Rate:** <5%
